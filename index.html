ITALIAN
DISHES
Italian food is known for its simplicity, quality ingredients, and bold flavors. Italian dishes often feature fresh, seasonal ingredients like tomatoes, garlic, onions, basil, oregano, rosemary, and olive oil. The emphasis is on the quality of the ingredients, rather than the complexity of preparation.
CHINESE
DISHES
Chinese cuisine is known for its balance and harmony of flavors, including sweet, sour, bitter, salty, and umami. It's also characterized by its use of fresh, seasonal ingredients, and a variety of preserved foods. Chinese food is rich and diverse, with different styles and tastes depending on the region, class, and ethnic background.
INDIAN
DISHES
Indian cuisine is known for its use of spices, vegetables, grains, and fruits, and its vibrant colors and rich flavors. Each region of India has its own cuisine, which reflects the demographics of the subcontinent. Indian cuisine is also known for its use of spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, cardamom, and cinnamon.

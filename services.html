Indian

Indian cuisine is known for its use of spices, vegetables, grains, and fruits, and its vibrant colors and rich flavors. Each region of India has its own cuisine, which reflects the demographics of the subcontinent.

♦ ♦

Italian

Italian food is known for its simplicity, ingredients, and bold flavors. Italian dishes often feature fresh, seasonal ingredients like tomatoes, garlic, onions, basil, oregano, rosemary, and olive oil. The emphasis is on the quality of the ingredients, rather than complexity of preparation.

♦ ♦

Chinese

Chinese cuisine is known for its balance and harmony of flavors, including sweet, sour, bitter, salty, and umami. It's also characterized by its use of fresh,seasonal ingredients, and a variety of preserved foods.
